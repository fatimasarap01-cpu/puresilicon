Our Story

It All Started with a Search.

We went looking for real silicone food storage bags, and couldn't find them. Just PEVA plastic, marketed as the real thing. So we built PureSilicon.

The Beginning

PureSilicon
Was Born

As a busy parent, our founder grew frustrated with the endless cycle of single-use plastic bags — buying, using, discarding, repeating. There had to be a better way.

After researching food-grade silicone and its remarkable safety profile, the idea for PureSilicon took shape: durable, reusable food storage that families could trust.

Our Mission

Making It Easy
to Reuse

We believe sustainability shouldn't mean sacrifice. Every PureSilicon product is designed for real life — meal prep, school lunches, leftovers, and everything in between.

No plastic, no PEVA, no compromises. Just pure, food-grade silicone that goes from freezer to microwave to dishwasher.

For You

For the
Food Lovers

Whether you're prepping meals for the week or packing snacks for an adventure, PureSilicon keeps your food fresh, sealed, and ready to go.

Our products are crafted for people who care about what they eat and how they store it — because good food deserves good storage.
