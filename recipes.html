Freezer Smoothie Packs

Prep a week's worth of smoothies in under 10 minutes. Portion banana, mixed berries, baby spinach, and your favourite extras into individual silicone bags, seal, and lay flat to freeze. Each morning, tip one bag straight into the blender with milk or yogurt — done in 60 seconds, no chopping required. A game changer for busy weekday mornings.

Overnight Marinated Chicken

Add chicken thighs, olive oil, lemon, garlic, and herbs to a silicone bag. Seal, massage to coat, and refrigerate overnight. The airtight seal infuses flavour far better than an open bowl.

🫧 Care Tip

January 2026

How to Clean Your Silicone Bags

Place bags open-side-up on the top rack. Silicone handles high heat, so the dishwasher removes grease and odours thoroughly. For stubborn smells, try a baking soda soak.

🧊 Care Tip

January 2026

Freezer Storage Done Right

Always leave a small gap at the top before sealing — liquids expand as they freeze. Lay bags flat while freezing so they stack neatly and thaw quickly under warm water.

Read Tip →
🌿 Care Tip

January 2026

Removing Odours from Silicone

Fill the bag with warm water and a tablespoon of baking soda. Leave for 30 minutes, then rinse. Leaving the bag in direct sunlight for a few hours also naturally neutralises odours.

Read Tip →
💧 Care Tip

December 2025

Storing Liquids & Soups

PureSilicon bags seal tight — ideal for soups, sauces, and dressings. Stand upright in the fridge or lay flat in the freezer. The silicone stays flexible even when frozen solid.
