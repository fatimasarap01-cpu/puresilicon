Your Data, Our Responsibility

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

At PureSilicon, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, and how we handle it — in plain language, not legalese. We operate in accordance with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA).

Information We Collect

When you shop with us or get in touch, we may collect:

  • Contact details — name, email address, phone number
  • Shipping details — delivery address
  • Order information — products purchased, order history, payment confirmation (we do not store full card numbers)
  • Communications — messages you send us via our contact form or email
  • Browsing data — pages visited, time on site, referring URL, device type (collected via cookies)

We only collect information that is reasonably necessary to provide our products and services.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process and fulfil your orders
  • Send order confirmations and shipping updates
  • Respond to your questions and support requests
  • Send marketing emails (only if you've opted in — you can unsubscribe any time)
  • Improve our website and product offerings
  • Comply with legal obligations

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

Sharing Your Information

We share your information only where necessary:

  • Shipping partners — to deliver your order (name and address only)
  • Payment processors — to securely handle transactions
  • Email service providers — to send order confirmations and newsletters
  • Analytics tools — to understand how our site is used (anonymised data)

All third-party providers we use are bound by confidentiality agreements and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — to remember your preferences, keep your cart, and understand how visitors use the site. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work as expected.

We do not use cookies to track you across other websites or sell your browsing data.

Your Rights

Under PIPEDA, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time
  • Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)

To exercise any of these rights, simply contact us at the address below.
