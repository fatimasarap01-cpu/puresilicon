Shipping & Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Shipping

We currently ship within Canada only. International shipping is coming soon.

Shipping Method Estimated Delivery Cost
Standard (Canada) 5–10 business days Calculated at checkout
Expedited (Canada) 2–4 business days Calculated at checkout
Free standard shipping on all Canadian orders over $75.

Processing time: Orders are processed within 1–3 business days of being placed. You'll receive a shipping confirmation with tracking once your order is on its way.

Please note that delivery times are estimates and may be affected by carrier delays, holidays, or weather. We're not responsible for delays once the package is in the carrier's hands.

Returns

We want you to love your PureSilicon products. If something isn't right, here's how we handle it.

  • We accept returns within 30 days of delivery.
  • Items must be unused and in their original condition and packaging.
  • We do not accept returns on items that have been used or washed.

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is defective or we made an error on your order.

Defective or Damaged Items

If your product arrives defective or damaged, we'll replace it for free — no questions asked.

  • Contact us within 30 days of receiving your order.
  • Include your order number and a brief description (or photo) of the issue.
  • We'll arrange a free replacement and cover any return shipping if needed.

How to Initiate a Return

Email us at hello@puresilicon.ca with:

  • Your name and order number
  • The item(s) you'd like to return
  • The reason for the return

We'll respond within 1–2 business days with instructions on how to proceed. Once we receive your return and confirm its condition, we'll process your refund within 5 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method.

Questions?

We're here to help. Reach us at hello@puresilicon.ca or visit our contact page.
