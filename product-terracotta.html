Product Information

The Terracotta 500ML Silicone Food Bag is crafted from premium platinum-cured silicone — the safest and most durable grade available. The wide-mouth opening makes it easy to fill, clean and dry, while the double pinch seal creates a secure, leak-proof closure.

  • Capacity: 500ML
  • Material: 100% platinum-cured food-grade silicone
  • Temperature range: –40°C to +230°C
  • Closure: Double pinch seal
  • Colour: Terracotta
  • Dimensions: approx. 18 × 14cm
  • Hand wash with warm soapy water, or place on the top rack of your dishwasher
  • Allow to air dry fully before sealing and storing
  • Do not use abrasive scrubbers or harsh chemical detergents
  • Safe for use in microwave, freezer and oven up to 230°C
  • Silicone may absorb strong odours over time — soak in baking soda water to refresh
